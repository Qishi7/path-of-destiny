Snake of Destiny 🐍
An interactive personal portfolio where the classic snake game serves as a metaphor for my life journey.

The Idea
Experience my transition from Biology to Mathematics through an interactive narrative. As you gather experience points in the game, you will face the four major crossroads that shaped my destiny.

Features
Interactive Choices: Play through pivotal life decisions.

Fate Correction: Witty feedback if your choices deviate from my actual path.

Full Timeline: A clean chronological summary revealed only after completing the game.

Academic Work: Direct link to my research publications.
