THE FULL JOURNEY

The Spark in High School
It started with a biology teacher whose passion made the world of cells and life feel like a masterpiece. You chose to dive into the organic complexity of the living world.
The Exploration in Undergraduate Years
Four years of rigorous laboratory work at sea and on land. While the experiments were fascinating you felt the call for something broader than the lab bench.
The Commercial Pivot in a Seven Year Career
You entered the professional world and proved your resilience and versatility. Seven years of success gave you a deep understanding of the market but you eventually hit the ceiling.
The Reinvention through Statistics Masters
You left your comfort zone and crossed the ocean. In the United States you traded stability for the rigorous logic of Statistics unlocking the power of data.
The Synthesis in Mathematics PhD
Today you combine the fragments of your past biology commerce and statistics into the deep abstraction of Mathematics. A journey from the organic to the logical.

"Your journey was never a calculation but a series of courageous leaps."
